ABOUT US

Managing a PG property can be overwhelming. That’s why we created PG Manager – an all-in-one solution designed to simplify your daily operations, enhance tenant experience, and keep your business running smoothly.

Our platform provides seamless automated rent collection, maintenance tracking, and tenant management, ensuring efficiency and peace of mind.

Whether you manage a single property or multiple PGs, our smart tools help reduce workload, improve transparency, and enhance profitability.

We are dedicated to helping you grow your business by minimizing manual tasks and allowing you to focus on what truly matters—your tenants' satisfaction.
